Welcome to PGDAV College

P.G.D.A.V. College, since its inception in 1957 has been a premier institution for higher learning. The college promotes academic excellence coupled with vibrant co-curricular activities to enable the holistic development of its students. It is one of the oldest constituent of University of Delhi and is located in the periphery of Lajpat Nagar. It offers eleven undergraduate courses and four post graduate courses.

At P.G.D.A.V. we believe in holistic education that goes beyond achieving academic excellence and vocational training. Our philosophy is to realize the overall intellectual, creative, cultural and socio-political growth and development of both the students and the college community in its entirety.
The College has a highly qualified and experienced faculty, well equipped computer labs, a library with over 70,000 books along with various journals and magazines and WiFi facilities that are available to the students everywhere within the campus. P.G.D.A.V. has also nurtured enormous sports talent in the field of cricket. College Alumni include international cricket players like Atul Wassan, Manoj Prabhakar and Raman Lamba who have brought laurels to the country. Moving into a new academic year P.G.D.A.V. College is committed to nurturing the talent of its students and equipping them with unbound skill and excellence.
